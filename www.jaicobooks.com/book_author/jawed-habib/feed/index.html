]]> A good hairdo boosts your confidence, and a major grooming battle is won if you have healthy hair with a cut that suits you. For help, here’s the man who introduced India’s masses to affordable hairstyling—Jawed Habib. Hailing from a family of renowned hairstylists—his grandfather was the appointed barber for India’s last Viceroy, Lord Mountbatten—Habib is among the country’s best-known hairdressers today.

He runs one of the largest unisex salon chains in India and is the ideal person to take you through the essentials of haircare. With more than three decades of grooming expertise behind him, in this book Habib busts popular myths about haircare and shows you how to:

  • Identify your hair type
  • Style your hair for various occasions
  • Choose suitable haircare products
  • Select nutritious foods for healthy hair
