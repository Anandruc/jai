]]> Everyone aspires to be a leader, but there is no fixed formula to become one. You may dream of leading a team but not know how to. Here is your chance to learn the ropes of leadership from an educator who has spent decades nurturing young minds into future leaders. In The Path to Leadership, author Kalyani Patnaik shares her observations and learnings acquired during her stellar teaching career to help her readers irrespective of their age or life experience.

This book shows you how to develop your individual talents, turn your weaknesses into strengths and transform yourself into a dynamic leader. The author believes that the right mix of traits such as intuition, industriousness, innovativeness and integrity could lead you to success. With multiple examples of famous celebrities across fields, you gain insight into how these traits have helped them flourish. The path to leadership may seem difficult but the goal is certainly attainable by moving in the right direction, and with this book you will master how to do it the right way.
