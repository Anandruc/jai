Once abused, abandoned, and forbidden to pursue her passion to train in the martial arts, she broke through the ultimate glass ceiling to become one of the most highly ranked martial artists in the world today and the founder of her own school of martial arts aimed at overcoming limitations in every area of life.

In Seven Steps to Inner Power Dr. Tae Yun Kim reveals how you can apply the secrets to break through every barrier to your own personal and professional goals.
