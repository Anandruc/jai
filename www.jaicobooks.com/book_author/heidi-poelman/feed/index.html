]]> It sounds simple enough. But staying in love is a whole lot different from falling in love. In a world of constant distraction, marriage experts agree that the little things matter a lot. Simple Secrets for Staying in Love explores the little things couples think, say and do to stay in love for the long haul. Supported by expert studies, interviews with happily married couples and years of personal experience, author Heidi Poelman shares the simple secrets that keep love alive. Whether it’s choosing an attitude of gratitude, leaving a simple love note, giving a welcome-home hug at the door or calling just to say “Hello,” Simple Secrets for Staying in Love is full of simple ways to love the one you married, two minutes at a time.
