There is a legend on earth about a mysterious eclipse that occurs every century. This is the time when at least a million people journey to find the God of Fortune. Only the brave who dare to confront their troubles will survive. Rich Legend is a story on how to make and save money the right way. In its chapters, you will find the secret to everlasting fortune. Set in ancient Egypt, it reveals how the wealthy have always done business, making the tale relevant to all modern-day readers.

This is the story of Magga, a young trader, who makes his fortune selling fermented fruit juice. After a profitable period, his business seems to slow down and the gold coins start to dwindle. Seeing that Magga has great potential, one of his loyal customers (a millionaire) gives him an old map and five golden coins inscribed with mysterious symbols.

The map will guide him to financial magicians; they alone can give him the wisdom to decipher the true value of his coins. But first, he must pass their tests!
